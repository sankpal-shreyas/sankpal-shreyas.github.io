# Hello, world

I'm Shreyas — backend engineer by trade, cybersecurity grad student at NYU Tandon, and somewhere in the margins a half-decent ML tinkerer.

This blog is the low-stakes place where those three interests rub against each other. Expect:

- **CTF writeups** from club events and weekend boxes.
- **Tooling notes** — when I build something small, I'd rather keep the notes here than in a dying scratchpad.
- **ML side-quests** — fine-tuning experiments, usually adjacent to security (think: classifying suspicious traffic, auditing prompt-injection defenses).

If any of that sounds interesting, stick around. If not, the projects page is probably more your speed.
